Challenges & Solutions

01

Before Digitalization

  • Manual & Fragmented Processes

    Service requests were handled via loose papers and phone calls with no central record.

  • Accountability Gaps

    Unable to track who was assigned to what, leading to delays and missing accountability.

  • Non-Existent Reporting

    Institutional performance was a "blind spot" with no data to drive service improvements.

02

After Implementation

  • Centralized Command & Control

    Single institutional ecosystem for all support requests, providing total transparency.

  • Automated Workflow & SLA

    Integrated logic for automatic routing, reminders, and resolution tracking against targets.

  • High-Fidelity Intelligence

    Board-ready analytics and real-time KPI monitoring to drive institutional growth.
